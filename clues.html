Homemade Clues

A fast, funny word-guessing game for two teams sharing one phone.

Bid on how few clues you'll need, then give single-word hints to race your team to the secret word. Bold bids win big — but overreach and you'll hand the round to the other side. Clue smart, bid low, win the round.

Launching soon on the App Store.

How it works

1

Place your bid

Each team bids how few one-word clues it will need to reach the secret word. Lowest bid wins the chance to play.

2

Give one-word hints

The clue-giver offers single words, one at a time, while their team races to guess before the clues run out.

3

Score and swap

Nail it within your bid to score. Miss, and the round swings to the other team. Then swap and go again.

Privacy

Overview. Homemade Clues is a party game designed for families. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data storage

Any settings or custom word lists you create stay on your device only. Nothing is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The app works entirely offline and asks for no special permissions. It does not access your contacts, photos, location, or microphone.

Children's privacy

The app is safe for players of any age. It collects no personal information and contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
