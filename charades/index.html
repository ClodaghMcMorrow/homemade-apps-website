Homemade Charades

One phone, two teams, three rounds of guessing.

The classic party game, reimagined for one phone and two teams. Fill your deck with famous people, movies and songs, then play it three times over — each round a trickier way to give clues. Playing with little ones? Kids mode swaps in picture cards and one simple round for children who can't read yet.

Launching soon on the App Store.

The three rounds

1

Round 1 · Describe

Say anything to get your team to the answer — just not the name itself, part of it, or a rhyme. Guess as many cards as you can before the timer runs out.

2

Round 2 · One word

The very same deck, but now a single word is your only clue for each card. You've seen them all once, so this round flies by.

3

Round 3 · Mime

No words at all — act it out, charades style. Whoever guesses the most cards across the three rounds wins.
